About Me

Erik Holden is a musical producer located in Charlotte, NC. He currently is producing music for local artists, but wants to broaden his music for artists around the world.

Erik has been producing beats for over two years and has created dozens of beats for all types of genres including hip-hop, trap, R&B, and pop music.
